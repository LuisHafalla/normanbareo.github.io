About Pokémon GO

Pokémon GO is a mobile game that allows players to catch Pokémon in the real world using augmented reality. Join millions of trainers around the globe and start your adventure today!

Features

  • Discover Pokémon in the real world
  • Join teams and battle in Gyms
  • Participate in special events
  • Trade Pokémon with friends
